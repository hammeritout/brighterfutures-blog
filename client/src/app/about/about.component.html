About Brighter Futures

Brighter Futures operates at the Lincoln Community Enrichment Center every Monday as a free Enrichment Program for the Wagoner community.
Brighter Futures was founded in March 2017 by Dr. Suzanne Salichs and Mr. John W. Jones.
Our goal is to complement and enhance our local schools to provide the best possible educational outcomes for the children and families of our community.
Brighter Futures is the only day program operating on Mondays in Wagoner. To date, we have served 200 school-aged children and over 55 volunteers have provided their time and talents.
